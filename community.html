A Safe Environemnt

To ensure a safe and healthy environment for all of our members, we have currently restricted access to Vitrine to members of select academic communities and universities. As our community grows, our number one priority will be to ensure a safe and trusting environment for you to buy and sell, without the fear of being misled or harassed, or otherwise inconvenienced.

Currently, only users with an email address belonging to one of the following universities are able to post videos to Vitrine. All posted videos are publicly accessible (you can remove them at any time), but contact information of the poster will also be only accessible to users with an email address belonging to one these universities:

  • Technical University of Munich
  • Ludwig-Maximilians University of Munich
  • Jacobs University
  • University of Freiburg

We are looking forward to expanding to other academic communities as well. If you want to use Vitrine to have a safe and secure trading environment at your university, please contact us at support@vitrine.cloud.
